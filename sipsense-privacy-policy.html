Privacy Policy for Sipsense

This Privacy Policy explains how Sipsense we collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-provided data (e.g., nickname, birthday, weight)
  • Cola intake data entered by users
  • Device information (only used for analytics, not linked to personal identity)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To visualize your cola intake history and progress
  • To provide personalized feedback and chatbot responses
  • For internal analytics to improve the app

3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored on our backend server hosted on AWS EC2 and managed via PostgreSQL. No third-party advertisers receive your data.

5. Children

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated in the future. Users will be notified within the app in case of major changes.
